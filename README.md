# 🎈 Ball TD 6
A Java tower defense game. Place towers, pop enemy balls, survive as many waves as you can.
---
## How to Play
Enemy balls spawn on the left and follow a fixed path toward the exit. Every ball that gets through costs a life. You have **20 lives** — lose them all and it's game over.
Between waves, spend your money on towers. Press `Space` or click **START WAVE** when you're ready.
---
## Controls
- `1` / `2` — switch tower type  
- **Click grass** — place selected tower  
- **Click tower** — upgrade it ($100)  
- `Space` or click **START WAVE** — start the next round
---
## Towers
- 🟫 **Dart Tower** — $50 · fast, can't pop Lead balls  
- ⬛ **Bomb Tower** — $150 · splash damage, the only way to pop Lead balls
Upgrade any tower for $100 to boost range and fire rate.
---
## Enemies
- 🔴 **Normal** — standard speed, appears every round  
- 🔵 **Speed** — fast, 1 health, shows up round 3+  
- 🟢 **Tank** — slow, 3 health, shows up round 4+  
- ⚪ **Lead** — dart-immune, needs bombs, shows up round 6+
---
## Money
- Start with **$50**  
- **+$1** per pop, **+$25** per wave cleared
---
## Game Over
When your lives hit 0, the Game Over screen shows your round and total pops. From there you can **Restart** or **Return to Menu**.
